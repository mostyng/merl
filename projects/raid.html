RAID (RISD Asian Intersections + Diaspora) is a student collective aiming to unite and mobilize Asian-identifying RISD students around issues of equity, representation, and politics in art and on campus.

We designed RAID’s identity, which forefronts a bold and unflagging typographic voice and color palette, as well as a seal logo to signify collectivity.

In our promotional art direction shots, the model resists eye contact, refusing to be objectified or commodified.
