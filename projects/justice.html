Branding, Art Direction, Editorial Design

Justice Is Global’s mission is to respond to rising international inequality as a result of neoliberal globalization.

JIG approached us to help them create a case statement: a printed piece to give context to their mission and articulate their goals. Our design approach was bold yet serious, highlighting the urgency needed to tackle structural inequality.

The organization aiming to end the “race to the bottom” in wages and working conditions on an international scale. JIG’s agenda is radical, targeting the root causes of issues like financial inequality, global warming, and unacceptable working conditions.
